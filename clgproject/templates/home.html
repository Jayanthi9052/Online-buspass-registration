ABOUT US

Online Bus Pass Registration is a web-based technology that will manage the records of the pass which is issue by administrative and also help to provide online bus pass to people who need to travel daily. Online Bus Pass Registration is helpful to bus administration by reducing the paperwork, time consumption and makes the process of getting bus passes as simple and fast.

Online Bus Pass Registration uses PHP and MySQL databases. This is the project which keeps records of the pass which is issue by the administrative.
